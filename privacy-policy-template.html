Privacy Policy for 17 WEEKS

Last Updated: December 19, 2024

Introduction

17 WEEKS ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

17 WEEKS collects the following types of information:

  • Weight Data: Your weight measurements and weight loss progress
  • Nutrition Data: Calorie intake, macronutrients, and food logs
  • Activity Data: Workout logs, exercise data, and activity tracking
  • Progress Photos: Photos you upload for progress tracking
  • Habit Data: Daily habits such as water intake, steps, and sleep
  • Profile Information: Name, age, height, and other profile details you provide

How We Store Your Information

All data collected by 17 WEEKS is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit, share, or store your personal data on external servers without your explicit consent.

Optional cloud backup features (if available) allow you to sync your data across devices, but this is entirely optional and requires your permission.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data stored locally on your device. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Services

17 WEEKS may use third-party services for analytics and crash reporting. These services may collect anonymous usage data to help us improve the app. No personally identifiable information is shared with third parties.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored in the app
  • Delete your data at any time by uninstalling the app
  • Export your data (if export features are available)

Children's Privacy

17 WEEKS is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
